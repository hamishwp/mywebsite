### Contact form
A simple form: **Name · Email · Subject · Message · Send**.

- On a static GitHub Pages site there is no server to send email, so the form posts to
  **[FormSubmit.co](https://formsubmit.co)** (free, no account): submissions are
  forwarded straight to **hamish.patten@gmail.com**.
- **Privacy:** the email is **never shown on the site or in the page source.** I'll use
  FormSubmit's **hashed/alias endpoint** (`https://formsubmit.co/<random-alias>`) so the
  raw address never appears in the HTML. To generate it, the first submission once
  triggers a one-time confirmation email — after that the alias is live.
- Niceties planned: a hidden `_subject` line ("New message from your website"), a
  honeypot field to reduce spam, and a "thank you" redirect after sending.
