# Visual Art

## Content
- Intro text to the page: "Visual Art has been part of my life for several years now, coming and going in waves. Here I show some recent work and an explanation of their meaning. As an Art Brute artist, I put more emphasis on the narrative of the painting than of the context of the work amongst the art world. Common themes in my work are self-loathing, nihilism, absurdism, homesickness and, more recently, AI-existentialism."

Have the text partially appearing below the painting, with the option to expand to read more. For mobile phones, make sure that there is only one painting at a time, then for laptops there are two paintings, side-by-side. For the text minimisation, have at least the title, the subtitle and 30 words of the 'text' column of the spreadsheet always there, but with the option to expand for the reader to read the entire text column field.
