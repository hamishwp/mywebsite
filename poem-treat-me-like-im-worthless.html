Treat Me Like I'm Worthless

Put it out on my skin Let me smell myself modify In the instant, there exists nothing more A mind of only pain and silence.

Tell me I’m nothing special, Tell me I’m nothing but ordinary, Let my mind know it’s futility to try to make me into something more.

Remove my skin with your nails, Reveal my inner self, Help me tear down the remains of who I wanted to be Then leave me, leave me with nothing.
